Living Outside the Box.

I've never fit into one category.
I started off studying computer engineering in University, but strived to use my creativity to make my mark on the world for the better.
Being an industrial designer allows me to use my analytical skillset to systematically dig deep down into the problems that need solving. I can also use my aesthetic and ergonomic sensabilities to develop well considered products.
Never being completely comfortable in one mode of thinking is a benefit for me and allows me to untangle wicked problems.
